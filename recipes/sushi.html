Sushi

Description

Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients, such as seafood.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Any topping as you prefer

Steps

  1. Simmer 80cc of vinegar, 20g each of sugar and salt, until they are dissolved.
  2. Cook the rice using a reduced amount of water compared to the usual method.
  3. Combine the rice and vinegar mix. Stir gently as if cutting with a spatula. Do not knead.
  4. Cool down the rice with fan to make it glossy.
  5. Gently shape the rice into an oval and place your toppings on it.
