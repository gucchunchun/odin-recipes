Miso soup

Description

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup consisting of a dashi stock into which softened miso paste is mixed.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Wakame
  • Japanese leek
  • Water
  • Dashi
  • Miso

Steps

  1. Cut tofu into 1.5cm cube. Cut japanese leek into small pieces.
  2. Add 1.5 cup of water with 1/3tsp of dashi in a small pot and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the tofu when the water boils and simmer for about 1minutes. Then add wakame in it.
  4. Dissolve 1tbsp of miso and add the leek and 1/3tsp of dashi, and mix.
